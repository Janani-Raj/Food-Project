PANEER TIKKA RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 3 tsp whole coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 2 tsp fennel seeds
  • 2 tsp white poppy seeds
  • 3-4 cloves peeled garlic
  • 1-inch piece of cinnamon
  • 1 whole star anise
  • 3-4 whole green cardamoms
  • 2 tsp whole black peppercorns
  • 1 teaspoon whole cloves
  • 10-12 dry Guntur red chilies

Process

Marinate the paneer and make the Chettinad masala paste. Press SAUTE button on the instant pot and follow the steps until adding water to the pot. Add Â¼ cup of water in place of 1 cup of water. Close the lid of the instant pot and set the valve to the sealing position. Press PRESSURE COOK and set the timer to 8 minutes at high pressure. Once the timer goes off, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minutes. Release the remaining pressure manually and open the lid of the pot. Add tamarind paste and cilantro and mix well. Serve hot.
